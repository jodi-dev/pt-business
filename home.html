About Us

Duane D. Physiotherapy - We have solutions for you!

Located on 665 Gaines School Rd, Athens, we serve our surrounding communities with a highly qualified professional team, that focuses in orthopaedic, nerological and gastric problems. Whether it be a sports-related injury or stroke, we have therapy plan tailored to your needs, to help you get back on track with your life.

Duane D. Physiotherapy associates may help you with:

  • An ADP Assessment
  • Sports Injury
  • Motor Vehicle Accident
  • Children's PT
  • Homeopathic PT
