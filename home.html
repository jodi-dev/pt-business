About Us

Duane D. Physiotherapy - We have solutions for you!

Located on 665 Gaines School Rd, Athens, we serve our surrounding communities with a highly qualified professional team, that focuses on orthopaedic and nerological problems. Whether it be a sports-related injury or stroke, we have therapy plan tailored to your needs, to help you get back on track with your life.

Duane D. Physiotherapy associates may help you with:

  • An ADP Assessment
  • Sports Injury
  • Motor Vehicle Accident
  • Children's Physiotherapy
  • Homeopathic Physiotherapy

Services

ADP Assessment

To be eligible for Assistive Devices Program devices, you need to be authorized by a qualified health care professional registered with the program. Our team provides this assessment service to our clients.

Sports Injury

If you exercised or played sports and sustained sprains, strains, or other soft tissue wounds, we can help you manage pain and discomfort with a physiotherapy plan.

Motor Vehicle Accident

Motor vehicle accidents are traumatic in nature, but our team of physiotheraipsts can help you improve from the physical and cognitive injuries. Book an appointment or consultation today to get evaluated for a treatment plan.

Children's Physiotherapy

We provide services for children in need of physiotherapy. We offer family-oriented sessions where the child may attend with their guardian, all at a discounted price. Book an appointment today to find out more!

Homeopathic Physiotherapy

Into naturalistic healing? We got you covered. Our specialists offer many inidividualistic blends of homeopathic remedies with physiotherapy science to help you heal spiritually and physically.
